About Margarita

Hi there! Glad you found me. You can call me Margarita or Megann.

I have been a Digital Marketing professional for nearly 10 years now, specialising in Social Media and Community Management, working in eCommerce, Telecommunications and Tech Startups. I bring to life campaigns that drive conversations and strengthen community (market) relations.

In 2021, I finally took the plunge and did something my 11-year-old self would be SO proud of: I started learning coding. You see, I was 11 years old when I made my first website. I learned everything by researching and studying on my own.

I love solving puzzles, I like fixing things, and ultimately, understanding how things worked.
